Imaging (pathology slide + CT) joined from the Imaging Data Commons DICOM_ALL table, collection tcga_kirc, on bcr_patient_barcode / PatientID — present for 112 of 518; CT series shown is the one with the most instances per study, as a proxy for the primary axial volume rather than a scout image. Driver mutations from MC3_MAF_V5_ONE_PER_TUMOR_SAMPLE, restricted to KIRC's six significantly mutated genes (VHL, PBRM1, SETD2, BAP1, KDM5C, MTOR) and excluding silent mutations — 268 of 518 have at least one. PTEN, AKT_pS473, and MTOR_pS2448 protein levels from TCGA_RPPA_PANCAN_CLEAN_FILTERED (reverse-phase protein array, log2-normalized), all three downstream of the VHL→HIF pathway this cancer is defined by — 451 of 518. Purity, ploidy, and genome-doubling status from PURITY_PLOIDY_ALL_SAMPLES_FILTERED (ABSOLUTE algorithm) — 330 of 518, useful context for how much to trust the calls above, since a low-purity sample dilutes every other signal.

Checked and deliberately left out: copy-number data for these six genes (ALL_CNVR_DATA_BY_GENE_FILTERED) — at most 1 patient per gene cohort-wide, essentially unusable.
